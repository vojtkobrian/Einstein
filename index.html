    A Time Line of Albert Einstein's Life:

  • 1879: Einstein was born in Ulm, Germany.
  • 1895: Fails entry exam for the Zurich Polytechnic and is accepted to the trade department of the contonal school in Aarau, Switzerland.
  • 1896: Einstein renounces German citizenship
  • 1900: Receives diploma as a mathematics teacher and starts work on doctoral thesis.
  • 1905: Completes paper on quantum theory.
  • 1921: Visits United States for the first time.
  • 1922: Wins Nobel Prize for work on quantum theory.
  • 1933: Moves to the United States.
  • 1939: Urges President Roosevelt for the acceleration of the atomic bomb.
  • 1955: Einstein dies in New Jersey.
